Electronic Torque Manager (ETM)

Our ETM has a solid track record on many vehicles, independent of their propulsion system, including one of the mostpowerful seriesproduction AWD BEVsportscars on the market. Its direct actuation method provides responsive and controllable electronically controlled driveline and axle coupling.

Key Features

  • Smooth and immediate engagement
  • Superior low drag torque performance
  • Excellent release response time and high control accuracy
  • Available as integrated or side-mounted variant

Key Technical Data

  • Max. Input torque: up to 12.500Nm
  • Max. Clutch Torque: up to 3.000Nm
  • Mechanically pre-emptive
  • Full and continuous lock-up feature is feature is available
